# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone, regardless of experience, identity, background, or personal characteristics.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, constructive, and technically honest.
- Focus criticism on code and decisions, not people.
- Respect privacy, especially when logs may contain device or process information.
- Do not publish secrets, tokens, private paths, or personally identifying captures.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, threats, discrimination, deliberate disruption, doxxing, and publication of private information are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behaviour violates these expectations.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub.
