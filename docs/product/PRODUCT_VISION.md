# MacPowerLab Product Vision

MacPowerLab is the battery-and-power equivalent of Activity Monitor, Task
Manager, and btop: useful immediately to a novice, detailed enough for an
engineer, and trustworthy enough for repeatable battery and charger analysis.

## Product pillars

1. **Live power monitor** — responsive battery, charger, component, thermal,
   and application-attribution views.
2. **Long-term history** — battery aging, charging behavior, app energy use,
   macOS changes, and charger comparisons over months or years.
3. **Repeatable benchmarks** — controlled CPU/GPU/memory workloads that expose
   peak battery draw, charger headroom, charge acceptance, and battery assist.

## Audience

- Novices should see plain language, safe defaults, normal guidance, and help.
- Advanced users should be able to inspect provenance, confidence, cadence,
  raw fields, contracts, logs, and benchmark details.
- No advanced capability should make the default experience unsafe or opaque.

## Long-term opportunity

With explicit consent and a privacy-preserving design, aggregated community data
could reveal battery-aging trends, charger behavior, macOS regressions, and apps
that consume disproportionate energy. No community upload is implemented until
an opt-in, previewable, anonymized telemetry contract is approved.
